Privacy Policy

Summary: Tabernacle collects location and account data to help you find and connect with prayer cells in your community. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Tabernacle ("we," "our," or "app") collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile details you choose to add or update
  • User Role: Your designated role in the app (Student, Leader, or Admin)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you use the map feature or enable location services. We use this to show nearby prayer cells and events.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, such as features used and screens visited

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core features (finding prayer cells, viewing events)
  • Show your location on the map to find nearby prayer cells
  • Allow leaders and admins to manage prayer cells and events
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates or notifications (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure app security

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power our app:

  • Google Maps: For map display and location features. Google's Privacy Policy applies to this usage.
  • JSONbin.io: For data storage and retrieval. Your data is stored securely on their servers.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety
  • Shared with your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time, and we will remove your data within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Location Permissions

The app requests location access to:

  • Show your position on the map
  • Find prayer cells near you
  • Calculate distance to nearby events

You can revoke location permissions anytime through your device settings. Note that some features may not work without location access.

7. User Roles and Permissions

The app has three user roles:

  • Student: Can view prayer cells, events, and their own profile
  • Leader: Can create and manage events, add prayer cells
  • Admin: Full access to manage users, events, and all prayer cells

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection

To exercise these rights, update your profile in the app or contact us directly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
